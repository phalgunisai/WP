Non-Veg Starters

Chicken Tikka

Succulent chunks of chicken are marinated in a harmonious blend of aromatic spices and creamy yogurt, infusing them with a burst of flavor. Slow-cooked to perfection in a traditional tandoor, these tender and smoky delights are a classic Indian favorite, served with a side of mint chutney.

Price per serving: $300

Seekh Kebab

Chicken is combined with aromatic spices and fresh herbs, then molded onto skewers and grilled to perfection. The result is a mouthwatering kebab with a smoky, charred exterior and a juicy, spiced interior.

Price per serving: $350

Tandoori Lamb Chops

Thick, juicy lamb chops are marinated in a blend of Indian spices and yogurt, then cooked in a tandoor for a smoky, aromatic flavor. The result is a tender, flavorful delicacy with a touch of spice.

Price per serving: $290

Fish Curry

Fish pieces are simmered in a fragrant curry made with an array of spices, coconut milk, and fresh herbs. This South Asian dish offers a perfect blend of spicy and savory flavors and is often served with rice or bread.

Price per serving: $250

Chicken Biryani

Fragrant, long-grain basmati rice is cooked with tender chicken pieces that have been marinated in a blend of spices. This one-pot wonder is garnished with fried onions and fresh mint leaves, creating a flavorful and aromatic meal.

Price per serving: $350

Sushi Rolls

Sushi rolls are a Japanese delicacy made with vinegared rice, fresh seafood (such as raw fish or cooked shrimp), and a variety of fillings like avocado, cucumber, and cream cheese. These rolls are wrapped in seaweed, known as nori, and then sliced into bite-sized pieces.

Price per serving: $370

Chicken Shawarma

Tender, marinated slices of chicken are stacked on a vertical rotisserie and slowly roasted until they become juicy and flavorful. The thinly shaved chicken is typically served in a warm pita bread or flatbread, accompanied by a variety of toppings like tahini sauce, cucumber, tomato, and onions. The result is a harmonious fusion of spices, herbs, and textures that is both satisfying and aromatic.

Price per serving: $150

Chicken Quesadillas (Mexican)

Flour tortillas are filled with seasoned chicken, melted cheese, and various toppings. Grilled until crispy, they are served with salsa and sour cream for a Tex-Mex delight.

Price per serving: $260

Chicken Satay (Thai)

Skewered chicken pieces are marinated in a fragrant blend of Thai spices and coconut milk, then grilled to perfection. Served with a creamy peanut sauce, they offer a harmonious blend of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors.

Price per serving: $300

Tempura Shrimp (Japanese)

Plump shrimp are coated in a light and crispy tempura batter and deep-fried to a golden perfection. Served with a soy-based dipping sauce, these shrimp provide a delightful contrast of textures.

Price per serving: $300
